About

With coding being the literacy of the 21st Century, we are bound to start teaching it while it's early, consequently teaching our students reading and writing. {Code} Town, an educational application for children that provides entertaining and engaging games while unconsciously learning about basic coding and developing their logical thinking skills.

{Code} Town is to design to be an an application that augment the learning process for the young learners in improving their logical thinking skills from conventional to an interactive platform. It will allow the child’s early development of logical and reasoning skills. It was developed as a game instead of a quiz or any lecture-based environment to let the preschoolers enjoy the games and gaining knowledge unconsciously, rather than having themselves thinking about the right answers to the questions they were given. Through {Code} Town, the team is hoping for a better and brighter future for the children and the world with the help of technology.


Our Mission

To provide a free, interactive, and educational application for children that is capable of developing their logical thinking skills necessary to establish fundamental foundations of understanding across different domains of knowledge.

Our Approach

By forming and providing a game design that can be extended to the traditional educational environment using blocks as visual programming tools to introduce logic and programming concepts to children; Theme customization and settings of games that specifically target the development of logical thinking skills.
